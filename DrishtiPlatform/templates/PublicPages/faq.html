Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the platform.

How do I register on Drishti? +

Click on the 'Register' button on the top right, select 'Citizen', and fill in your details including mobile number and address. You will receive an OTP for verification.

How can I track my complaint? +

Go to the 'Track Complaint' page and enter the unique Complaint ID provided to you via SMS/Email. You can also view status in your dashboard after logging in.

What if my complaint is not resolved? +

If the resolution time exceeds the SLA, the complaint is automatically escalated to higher authorities. You can also manually escalate it from your dashboard if you are unsatisfied with the resolution.

Is my personal data secure? +

Yes, Drishti uses end-to-end encryption and follows strict government data privacy protocols. Your personal details are only visible to authorized officers handling your case.

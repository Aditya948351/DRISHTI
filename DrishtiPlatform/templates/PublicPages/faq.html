Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the platform.

How do I register on Drishti?

Click on the 'Register' button on the top right, select 'Citizen', and fill in your details including mobile number and address.

How can I track my complaint?

Go to the 'Track Complaint' page and enter the unique Complaint ID provided to you via SMS/Email.

What if my complaint is not resolved?

If the resolution time exceeds the SLA, the complaint is automatically escalated to higher authorities. You can also manually escalate it from your dashboard.

About Drishti

Revolutionizing Governance through Technology

Purpose

Drishti is designed to be the eyes of the administration, providing clear visibility into citizen grievances. Our mission is to create a responsive ecosystem where complaints are not just filed but actively resolved through intelligent workflows and automated accountability measures.

Scope

Civic Issues

Roads, Water, Sanitation, Streetlights

Administrative

Delays, Corruption, Service Denial

Law & Order

Public Nuisance, Safety Concerns

Technology

Built on a robust Django framework, Drishti integrates advanced AI/ML models to automatically categorize complaints, detect duplicates, and predict resolution timelines.
